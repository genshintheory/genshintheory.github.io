Luka is similar to Kafka in how his debuff works. However, Kafka is better in applying her shock debuff in aoe situations, whereas Luka is more single target.

The 24% hp in Luka's E is a bit deceptive. The bleed damage is the minimum of 24% of enemy max hp and a portion of Luka's atk. So for enemies with low max hp, bleed may do less absolute damage than for high max hp enemies. This will be the case until a certain critical point where 24% max hp is equal to the atk scaling limit of the bleed debuff.

By contrast, Kafka's shock debuff just scales with her atk, without having this hp restriction. It does the same absolute damage regardless of enemy hp.
