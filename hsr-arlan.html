Arlan is an electro destruction character that uses a sword in battle. He is perhaps a prototype for what a continuous hp-draining destruction character looks like in Star Rail. In Genshin, Xiao was the original destruction character with continuous hp drain for years until Fontaine came.

His E drains his hp to do more damage. His E does not cost sp (but does not generate any). So he has a choice of using his ba or e (drain hp). His Q has 110 energy cost and does a blast nuke.
