Calculations

Level 10 q has total scaling $$s_q = 158.4 + 8(43.2) + 339.84 = 843.84$$ It costs 40 energy and has 12s cd. Assuming 61.6 electro dmg bonus for Qing (e.g. 2tf) the dps is $$x_q = 1.616s_q/12 = 113.63712$$

Level 10 e has total scaling $$s_e = 90.72 + 302.4 = 393.12$$ This is for doing double e to get infusion. (If one does e then ca detonation scaling is \(90.72 + 2(151.2)\)). Note c1 adds extra instance of damage to double e, but we are considering c0 here.

The e has a 7.5s cd. The dps is $$x_e = 1.616s_e/7.5 = 84.704256$$ She gains an electro infusion from this ability and can also plunge attack with it.

Total dps from e and q only is $$x_e + x_q = 198.341376$$
