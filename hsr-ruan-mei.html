Ruan Mei (阮梅), released in 1.6. Ruan (阮) corresponds to Nguyen in Vietnam.

Ruan allows the team to deal more gauge toughness damage. Similar to Silver Wolf, she has no parallel in Genshin. However, Ruan's kit is a bit easier to translate into Genshin. In Genshin, most attacks will deal 1A, 2B, 4C gauge units of damage, with some things like Nahida's E applying 1.5 units. In the source code, gauge is referred to as 'durability'.
