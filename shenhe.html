Shenhe (申鹤) is a Cryo support character.

Talents

Her buffs scale with her ATK.

Build

2 piece Glad, 2 piece Shimenawa OR 4 piece Blizzard Strayer.

ATK/ER sands, ATK/Cryo DMG goblet, ATK/CRIT circlet. Substats: ATK, CRIT > ER.

Calamity Queller, Skyward Spine, PJWS, Homa, Deathmatch, Blackcliff, Catch, Crescent Pike, Starglitter.

Teams

She can support Eula, Ayaka, Ganyu teams. She can be used on Physical, Freeze, reverse melt teams.
