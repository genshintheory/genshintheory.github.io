Talents

Her E sacrifices her health and creates an electro ring that heals you. Her Q does dot.

For artifacts, one can use 4p tenacity, 4p gilded (for em), noblesse, ocean clam, etc. One can do full HP build, EM build, electro-crit, or hybrid build.

As part of the Arataki gang, does she synergize with Itto? She can heal Itto for sure. But note that some of Itto's best teammates are not necessarily from the Arataki gang (i.e. Miss Hina ahem Gorou).
