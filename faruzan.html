Faruzan is a new anemo character that will debut in 3.3. She works in the Haravatat Darshan (one of the six Darshans of the Akademiya) and has been a herbad (mentor) for over a century. In some ruins she encountered something which stopped her from aging.

Voice actor:

  • English: Chandni Parekh
  • Chinese: Yan Mengmeng (阎萌萌)

Talents

Her AA is called 'Parthian shot', which is also the name of a cavalry tactic used by the Parthians of Persia. The Parthian empire existed in Persia after Alexander's Hellenic empire.

After using her E (Nasamjnin), her next aimed shot will create a vortex that can clump enemies. It can also mark enemies (and allies) and create a vacuum vortex. With her passive, the arrow can also shred anemo res.

With her Q, she will create a polyhedron that releases whirlwind pulses, which reduce anemo res and buff anemo dmg to nearby party members. Another passive buffs anemo damage based on Faruzan's base attack.

Teams

Faruzan can support anemo dps like Xiao and the new character Scaramouche. It looks like the game is getting an anemo buffer, similar to Shenhe (cryo), Gorou (geo), Sara (electro).
