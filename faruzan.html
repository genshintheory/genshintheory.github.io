Faruzan is the best anemo buffer in the game, debuting in v3.3. She works in the Haravatat Darshan (one of the six Darshans of the Akademiya) and has been a herbad (mentor) for over a century. In some ruins she encountered something which stopped her from aging. She also like geometry.

Voice actors:

  • English: Chandni Parekh
  • Chinese: Yan Mengmeng (阎萌萌)

Talents

Her AA is called 'Parthian shot', which is also the name of a cavalry tactic used by the Parthians of Persia. The Parthian empire existed in Persia after Alexander's Hellenic empire.

After using her E (Nasamjnin), she gains the manifest gale effect for 18s. During this state, her next aimed shot will create a vortex that can clump enemies. It can also mark enemies (and allies) and create a vacuum vortex (pressurized collapse). With her passive, the pressurized collapse can also shred anemo res for 4s (applies perfidious wind), and the charging time is reduced by 60%.

With her Q, she will create a polyhedron that releases whirlwind pulses, which reduce anemo res (perfidious wind) and buff anemo dmg bonus for nearby party members. Another passive buffs anemo addtivec damage based on 32% of Faruzan's base attack. Her Q lasts 12s.

Constellations

C1 allows her to fire two hurricane shots during her E manifest gale state. C2 extends Q duration by 6s to 18s. C4 gives energy. C6 gives 40% anemo crit dmg during Q and will allow characters to apply pressurized collapses (vacuum cc) on enemies every 3s.

Teams

Faruzan can support anemo dps like Xiao and the new character Scaramouche. It looks like the game is getting an anemo buffer, similar to Shenhe (cryo), Gorou (geo), Sara (electro).

Review

Faruzan is the best anemo buffer in the game, and she is only a 4 star.

For one thing, the chad Faruzan shreds 30% anemo res at c0. By comparison, the virgin zhongli can only shred 20% even though he is a 5 star. To kick people when they are down even more, ganyu can only shred 15% cryo res with c1: ganyu as a 5 star needs constellations to do less than what Faruzan can do at c0!

Faruzan is still and a top support for anemo dps like Xiao and Scaramouche. For Xiao specifically, Faruzan is almost a must for any optimal team and is close to Bennett in terms of the overall buff she provides for Xiao.

Given Xiao's top dps, Faruzan is a game changer, a true meta-reshaping support that brings Xiao to the next level. Who would have thought that it was possible to make a god go any higher.
