Scaramouche passive

Scaramouche has a passive called Gales of Reverie where the probability of activating wind arrows increases with each NA/CA hit. The base probability is 16% per hit. If a hit does not activate the wind arrows, the probability increases by 12%.

The question becomes: how does one calculate dps from this? What is the expected damage from this? It turns out this uses a markov chain. We will call such effect 'markov chain effects' (MC effect). The EO set from the chasm is another example of a markov chain effect.

To define the markov chain, start with the initial state 0. With probability .16 one can transition from 0 back to 0 (activate the wind arrow). With probability .84 one will transition to state 1 (with 12% increased probability to proc wind arrow).

In state 1, with probability .28 one will transition back to state 0 (activate the wind arrow, reset probability). With probability .72 one will transition to state 2 (with 12% increased probability to proc wind arrow). And so on.

From this, one can define a transition matrix P where \(P_{ij}\) is the probability of transitioning from state i to j. For the above this matrix looks like: $$P = \begin{pmatrix} .16 & .84 & \\ .28 & 0 & .72 & \\ .4 & 0 & 0 & .6 & \\ .52 & 0 & 0 & 0 & .48 & \\ .64 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & .36 & \\ .76 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & .24 & \\ .88 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & .12 \\ 1 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 & 0 \end{pmatrix}$$

After 7 missed hits, the 8th hit is guaranteed to proc the wind arrow.
