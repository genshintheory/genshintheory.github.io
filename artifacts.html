Artifact Domain Ranking

  1. Emblem domain (tie for first): both emblem and shimenawa (2 piece) sets can be used on various characters. Emblem is bis for many, and 2 piece shimenawa (givint 18% ATK) can also be used on various dps characters such as Xiao, Yae, etc. The 2p shimenawa is essentially farmable gladiators.
  2. Chasm domain (tie for first): both 2p sets of Vermillion and EO give ATK, making them universal on most characters in the game. The 2p-2p combo works well on most characters too.
  3. Gilded domain (tie for first): Gilded works on many characters as a universal EM set. Deepwood can also buff dendro damage. Although deepwood lags compared to VV, Gilded carries this domain and makes up for deepwood's drawbacks and problems. And what is the choice of 14% for? To remind us of a corrupt person who got jailed for 14 years? It almost makes it look like Star Rail needed Qingque to compensate for that, or the Toledo weapon needed a 14s effect, or baizhu needed a 14s burst to apologize for that.
  4. Viridescent Venerer: vv gives 40% res shred, which is a big buff to damage, making it one of the most important sets for anemo supports, which many teams need. Even though maiden's is the other set in this domain, the damage increase from the anemo res shred basically offsets this.
  5. Dragonspine domain: both sets are pretty good. The blizzard strayer is the bis for freeze cryo units. The hydro set 2p is also pretty good, and the 4p is bis for Childe. However, many other hydro units have different bis (like emblem or clam).
  6. Husk/clam domain: both sets are very niche for specific units in the game that not everybody may use. But husk is bis for those specific units (Albedo, Itto, etc). And the clam set is a nice idea for converting healing into damage.
  7. Crimson domain: crimson witch is still valuable for pyro dps in absence of other pyro artifacts. But the pyro res artifact on the other hand is underwhelming.
  8. Noblesse domain: Noblesse is good, but took a hit since Emblem was released in 2.0. Noblesse is still valuable but not to the extent that it was before version 2.0. Bloodstained can be easily replaced by Pale Flame.
  9. Desert/Paradise Lost: Desert is a decent set, but Paradise drags this domain down. Creating redundancy to make political attacks on others is looked down upon. Domain ranks above tenacity as at least it is named city of gold.
  10. Tenacity/Pale Flame domain: both sets are very niche for specific units in the game. Even for those units, they can basically be replaced. With more HP scaling units though, the value of this domain can rise.
  11. Vourukasha/Nymph domain (tie with Tenacity): so 'vourukasha' is not technically the exact translation of the Chinese term, something like 'Amrita' would be closer. Other than that, Nymph gets penalized for trying to create redundancy like the Paradise Lost set.

Guide

  • VH (Chensha): Xiao (bis)
  • Gilded: Yae, Cyno, DMC, Tigh (all bis), Kuki (full EM)
  • VV: any anemo character
  • Deepwood: any support character (usually dendro), but may not be bis
  • Blizzard: any cryo character
  • Crimson: any pyro dps
  • Husk: def scalers such as Itto, Yun Jin, Albedo, Noelle, Gorou
  • Heart of Depth: Childe (4p), Ayato, Nilou (2p)
  • Emblem: Mona, Raiden, Yelan, XQ, XL, Beidou (bis), Chongyun, Kaeya, Rosaria, etc. (for maximizing burst dmg)
  • Noblesse: Bennett, Jean, many characters can use 2p for burst dmg
  • WT: Ganyu (melt), Tigh
  • Shimenawa: Yoimiya, Ganyu (melt)
  • Gladiator: Cyno, Razor, Ayato
  • EH: Yoimiya, Razor, Ayato (not bis for any though)
  • Tenacity: hp scalers such as Yelan, Candace, Nilou, Ayato (2p), Kuki, Kokomi, Morax (4p)
  • Pale Flame: Eula, Razor
  • Clam: Qiqi, Kokomi, Barbara
  • TF: Cyno, Fischl, Keqing
  • Thundersoother, Petra, Bolide, Lavawalker: never use these
