Jing Yuan (JY) in Genshin

The key is to replicate the lightning lord (LL). In Genshin, JY can have a passive where the LL deals damage say every 40s. By default it deals 3 hits of damage with say 100% scaling each. When JY uses E, it does an aoe slash that increases the LL hits per action by 2 while decreasing its cd by 10s. When JY uses Q, it does a larger aoe slash with higher scaling that increases the LL hits per action by 3 while decreasing its cd by 20s. The max hits per action is 10 (at c0). The E can have a 10s cd, and the Q can have 60-80 energy.
