Xingqiu (行秋) is a Hydro support character and friend of Chongyun.

Talents

Elemental Burst

The reason Xingqiu is used is for his off field Hydro application through his burst.

Constellation Lv. 6 increases the damage of every third sword rain attack during this Talent's duration (creates 5 sword rains). Xingqiu regenerates 3 Energy when the third Rain Sword attack hits enemies. Normally, sword rain alternates between firing 2 swords and 3 swords. With C6, the order is 2 swords → 3 swords → 5 swords. There is usually a 1s delay between successive waves. The burst lasts 15s.

Without any Constellations, this skill at Talent Level 10 has a scaling of 97.7% per sword. On average, there are 25 instances of Rain Sword DMG, resulting in a total scaling of 2442.5%. At C6, this skill at Talent Level 13 has a scaling of 115% per sword. On average, there are \(10*5 = 50\) instances of Rain Sword DMG, resulting in a total scaling of 5750%.

We assume Xingqiu has a Hydro DMG Bonus goblet. He gets 20% Hydro DMG through his passive. The total DMG Bonus on the burst is $$46.6 + 20 = 66.6$$ The burst has a 20 second cd, so the DPS at C0 is $$x_0 = 1.666*2442.5/20 = 203.46025$$ and the DPS at C6 is $$x_6 = 1.666*5750/20 = 478.975$$ Note this is really all single target damage. Other characters will have a greater advantage in AoE situations.

Childe and Xingqiu are sometimes compared. A C0 Childe can do more damage than a C0 Xingqiu. At C6, Xingqiu may be competitive in single target damage with a C0 Childe. However, Childe has better AoE, has Riptide, and his Hydro application does not cost energy whereas Xingqiu's Hydro application does.
