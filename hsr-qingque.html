Much of Qingque's damage is based on rng. She is a gambler type of character, similar to the Cait Sith in FF7 or Setzer in FF6, relying on chance and luck. The game Qingque plays is mahjong / majiang (麻将), a Chinese tile game. This game is also called maque (麻雀), using the same 雀 in Qingque's name. Que (雀) means sparrow.
