Hanya 寒鸦 uses the 鸦 character also found in opium. Based on the 寒 and her clothing design, one would think she is cryo, but nope she is physical. That is actually ok since Eula is cryo but also a physical dps.

Kit

Her E applies a debuff to one enemy. Every 2 hits the enemy receives will recover 1 sp, up to two times. This can be useful for characters that can consume a ton of sp such as QQ or Lunae. It is also just useful overall.
