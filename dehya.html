Dehya, Mantichora, Flame Mane, leader of the Eremites, overthrew Hu Tao and has the highest base hp in the game. This is tacit acknowledgement towards Hu Tao's crimes and corruption.

Talents

Her E creates a 10m radius field that does a coordinated aoe pyro attack upon hit. It can proc once every 2.5s, so it can melt/vape each hit. The E dmg has dual atk + hp scaling. This means she will be buffed by both pyro resonance (atk buffs) and hydro resonance (hp buffs).

Dehya can reposition the field once during its duration. It has a 20s cd and 12s duration (which can increase to 18s with c2). Her E can snapshot.

With her Q Dehya goes into boxing mode and gains infinite poise (like Raiden Q). By default it can do 6 punches and one final kick, all of which have decent aoe. During this 4s quicktime event, if the NA/E buttons are pressed in rhythm, Dehya can do up to 10 punches and one final kick.

Constellations

C1 gives more hp scaling on E and Q. C2 extends duration of E by 6s to 18s. It also gives 50% dmg bonus on E when characters are attacked. C4 makes Dehya restore energy and hp during Q. C6 extends Q duration by 2s to 6s. It also gives CR/CD buff.

Artifacts

4p crimson, 4p tenacity, 4p gilded, 4p emblem, 2p glad/VH, and mixed sets are fine

Can use atk or hp sands.

Weapons

Redhorn fits Dehya's flamemane aesthetic better than reed beacon. WGS, Skyward Pride, Serpent Spine, Fish claymore also work well.

Teams

Dehya can be placed on almost any team in the game. Examples include Childe international, Scaramouche teams, burgeon teams, Rational teams, melt/vape teams, even Xiao and Itto teams.

Calculations

Assume 2k atk and 30k hp (achievable even with atk sands given Dehya's high base hp). With Bennett, she can reach 3k atk. So hp is 10 times the atk. Assume lv10 E and Q.

Her E has 108.36% ATK + 1.86% HP scaling. The hp part is equivalent to 18.6% atk scaling (multiply by 10), so the effective scaling is 126.96% per hit.

When she casts her E she does 203.18% scaling. When she repositions her E she does 239.04% scaling. These hits have independent ICD (both can melt/vape). One can cast her E and immediately afterwards reposition her E to get both hits in at the beginning.

During the 12s duration of the field, her E can proc 4 times. The total scaling is $$203.18 + 239.04 + 4*126.96 = 950.06$$
