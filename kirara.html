Combat

Kirara's E creates a dendro shield. Her Q does a dendro nuke that creates smaller bomblets like Aloy's skill. These bomblets can be clustered and pulled via anemo vacuum abilities like Sucrose E.

Using hold E will generate the shield and turn Kirara into a box that can move around fast, jump, and even climb up walls. Her movement speed, jumping, climbing speed, and stamina consumption while climbing are all increased. This state lasts up to 10s.

The shield lasts 12s. The press cd is 8s (so the shield can have 100% uptime, unlike baizhu). By holding E the cd can be longer up to 12s. Note the cd starts after leaving the box cat state (so she can run into the Childe dilemma).

Moreover, using the E again while the shield is active will stack the shield and make it absorb more damage up to a certain max limit.
