As of 1.4, against 4-5 or more enemies, Jing Yuan does not really have any competition. His E and Q are all aoe. His LL hits are blasts that can hit all enemies. No other character has such easy access to true aoe and high scaling.

Blade's passive is aoe and quite good. If he could proc it more often (2 or 3 stacks instead of 5), it would be better. Himeko's E is a blast, not aoe. And her passive (3 stacks) does not proc often enough. Herta and Clara do have aoe E but do less damage than JY. Lunae does not have true aoe, only blasts. Seele is actually decent in aoe due to her passive reset. Kafka's burst is aoe and decent.

As Lunae does not have true aoe and can only do blasts (2-3 enemies), Jing Yuan is not his competition. Lunae's true competition includes other destruction characters such as Blade or Clara as well as Seele in the domain of 2-3 enemies. So it makes more sense to compare Lunae to Blade and Seele.

Update: in 1.5, Argenti (Ginshi) is quite good at aoe too.

Stat distribution

Stats like dmg bonus, crit appear to be more ample in HSR than in Genshin. Supports like Tingyun, Bronya, Ruan Mei, Yukong can give a bunch of dmg bonus and crit. JY's signature weapon (Before Dawn) gives a free 48% dmg bonus to fua. Bronya E gives another 66% dmg bonus, so it can be easy to reach 100%+ dmg bonus for free. What this means is that dmg bonus is no longer as rare in HSR as it is in Genshin. As such, having more of other stats such as atk may actually lead to a dps increase.
