Talents

Has an E like Childe/Hu Tao/Yoimiya and a Q like Ganyu.

Burst

Here come the Ganyu simps. But this has a more expensive 80 energy cost and 2s downtime. Sub-dps is unreliable even with Venti as there is anti-synergy. Ayato q lasts 18s, whereas Venti's q lasts less time, so enemies will not be cc'd the whole time, meaning there will be more inefficiency (not every raindrop will hit).

Ayato has a passive similar to Barbara, which will regenerate energy over time up to 40/80. It is an interesting symbolic choice given the voice actors.

Passive

He makes sussy food. Here come the Hu Tao simps.

Teams

Xiangling: however since Ayato's E only lasts 6s whereas XL's Q lasts 10-14s, Ayato does not fit Xiangling as well as Childe, who can keep up for the duration of XL's burst

Yun Jin: she can buff Ayato's NA, but Ayato has an HP self-buff so both the EO set and YJ buff will hit diminishing returns. Yun Jin's damage increase is more effective for characters like Yoimiya or Razor. That said, the other buffs from Yun Jin's constellations (C6) can still help Ayato.

Anemo buffers: Venti, Kazuha, Sucrose to provide CC and VV shred

Electro characters for EC teams, but in version 2.6, Genshin said the EC reaction had a bug for Ayato

Cryo for freeze teams

Ayato does not have a tailor-made support or even a tailor-made set for that matter.

Calculations

Ayato's E (镜花 Jinghua) lasts 6s and has a 12s cd. Within its duration, he can do 15 NA (the 3-hit combo 5 times). For a level 10 E, the dps is $$x=(349.35*5)*(1.466+.2)/12 = 242.507125$$ The .2 is from the 20% NA damage from a level 10 Q. Dps is meh. Childe has higher dps with riptide. And Ayato's hydro application is worse with the icd, so Ayato is a worse enabler. The AoE is fair, but Ayato looks like a literal stone 石 while auto-attacking. During his E, Ayato cannot CA or PA. During Childe's E, at least he can CA and has riptide for hydro application. Moreover, Childe has the freedom to choose the duration. He can choose it short at 6s like Ayato or he can last longer up to 30s to accomodate other characters like Xiangling. Meanwhile, Ayato is stuck at 6s or less.

Ayato also has a water illusion that explodes once with 200.6% scaling. He also has the Namisen HP scaling. Level 10 E gives 1.11% HP per stack, max 4 stacks at C0 (at C2 becomes 5). He gains 1 stack every .1s. He attacks every 6/15=.4s. One of his passives gives 2 stacks at the start. Illusion explosion gives max stacks. So assume he has max 4 stacks the whole time. Assume he has 30k hp (if he has an ATK sands this may be harder to achieve) and 2k atk (Ayato is on the lower end of attack at 299, lower than Tatar). Max 4 stacks is 4.44% of hp, which is 1332 bonus damage. Scaling on shunsuiken (纯水剑) is around 100% each (actually a bit more) at level 10. Factoring in Bennett gives say 3000 attack, so base damage is around 3000. So the hp scaling gives roughly 4332/3000=1.444 times increase in damage $$1.444x = 350.1802885$$ This is Keqing level. Then add illusion explosion $$(200.6)*(1.466)/12=24.5066333333$$ The dps goes to around 375 mv. Yoimiya does more damage (and has higher base attack).
