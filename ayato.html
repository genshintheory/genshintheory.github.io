Childe, Ayato, Noctis

How do these three main hydro dps compare? Childe has quadratic riptide and becomes better the more enemies there are within a concentrated aoe. He scales purely with atk. Ayato's (Otto's) aoe is decent, but what makes him different is his off-field application. He dual-scales with atk and hp. Noctis has great aoe and very high natural damage too. He scales purely on hp. Childe, and Ayato, are also reliant on Bennett to maximize their dps, whereas Noctis is not (scaling with hp).

Childe and Noctis have higher dps on-field, whereas Ayato can be used more for off-field. XQ, Yelan have better off-field hydro app, so Ayato is somewhere in between Childe and Yelan in terms of on-field/off-field playstyle. So he tends to run into the jack of trades, master of none situation.

So the question becomes: who is the ultimate hydro dps, Childe or Noctis? For general situations, when enemies are spread out or far away, and even in single-target, Noctis can be a top hydro dps. Noctis is also not burdened by potentially long cooldowns. However, Childe tends to shine more when there are many enemies (3+) within a concentrated aoe, even outdamaging Noctis in those cases. Childe also tends to synergize better with vape teams involving XL.
