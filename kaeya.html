Kaeya Alberich has higher NA scalings than Ayaka.

Talents

E does cryo spray. Q creates cryo ring around character that does off-field dps. With his passive, E heals Kaeya for 15% of his atk. With another passive, freezing opponents with E can generate up to 2 extra particles.

C1 increases NA/CA crit rate by 15% against opponents affected by cryo. C2 get extend Q duration from 8s to 15s (extends 2.5s for each opponent defeated during Q). C4 creates shield when Kaeya's hp falls below 20% lasting 20s (cd 60s). C6 makes Q create 1 more icicle and restore 15 energy.

The C4 is a destruction / preservation kind of constellation, similar to Xiao's C4 buffing defense. The passive that allows Kaeya to heal himself with E also gives him additional survivability.

Build

Can use 4p blizzard, 4p noblesse, 4p emblem, and mixed sets.

Teams

Kaeya is usually used on quickswap melt/freeze teams. For example, Dehya-Kaeya-Rosaria-Bennett or Kaeya-Chongyun-Yelan-Anemo. He can be played as a physical carry given his high NA scalings: Kaeya-Mika-Electro. He can also be paired alongside Eula or Ayaka.

There is the Kaeluc team (Diluc melt team): Diluc-Kaeya-Bennett-Anemo (Sucrose, etc.).
