Kaeya Alberich has higher NA scalings than Ayaka.

Talents

E does cryo spray. Q creates cryo ring around character that does off-field dps.

Build

Can use 4p blizzard, 4p noblesse, 4p emblem, and mixed sets.

Teams

Kaeya is usually used on quickswap melt/freeze teams. For example, Dehya-Kaeya-Rosaria-Bennett or Kaeya-Chongyun-Yelan-Anemo. He can be played as a physical carry given his high NA scalings: Kaeya-Mika-Electro.

There is the Kaeluc team (Diluc melt team): Diluc-Kaeya-Bennett-Anemo (Sucrose, etc.).

Calculations

As Kaeya is one of the starter characters alongside Amber and Lisa, it may take a longer time to get c6 Kaeya. But here we still assume lv13 E and Q. Also assume 4p blizzard strayer for 61.6% cryo dmg.
