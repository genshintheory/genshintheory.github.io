The numerical effect of Bennett's buff on the DPS will be roughly the same for most characters, so Bennett is not factored into most of these calculations. The exceptions such as Hu Tao or Itto are handled on a case by case basis. In these cases, the DPS/MV is scaled after taking into account the relative effect of Bennett's buff on their ATK.

Note: Beidou gets +3 levels on Q at C5. Xiangling gets +3 levels on Q at C3. For Beidou's case, the DPS per enemy is given. (For most other AoE abilities besides Beidou, the DPS per enemy is equal to the DPS.)
