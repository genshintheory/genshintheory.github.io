General overview of gameplay mechanics of characters

Support

Sometimes the best defense is offense, and the best offense is defense.

Buffers/Offensive supports:

ATK Buffer: Bennett, Sara, any catalyst user with TTDS, Yoimiya (after using Q), certain constellation effects, etc.
DMG increase buffer: Yun Jin, Shenhe
DMG Bonus buffer: Mona, Kazuha
DEF buffer: Gorou
Talent scaling buffer: Childe (passive increase NA level +1)
CRIT buffer: Rosaria, Sara C6, Gorou C6, Shenhe C2
Reaction buffer/enabler: Xingqiu (hydro applier, vaporize), Childe (hydro applier, vaporize), Sucrose (EM), Kazuha (C2)
RES shred buffer: any Anemo VV unit (Venti, Jean, Kazuha, Sucrose), Morax, superconduct units, Shenhe (Q), Eula (E)
Crowd Control (Vacuum): Venti, Kazuha, Sucrose, Jean, Anemo MC (AMC)
Battery: Raiden, Electro MC (EMC), usually bring corresponding element
Other: Chongyun (cryo infusion for shield shred)

Defensive supports:

Healer: Jean, Qiqi, Barbara, Kokomi, Diona, Bennett, Noelle, Gorou C4 (not much), Morax C6 (not much)
DMG reducer: Jean (C6), Xingqiu, Beidou
Shield: Diona, Xinyan, Thoma, Beidou (C1), Yanfei (C4), Morax, Noelle, any Geo character via crystallize

Off field dps

DoT should last for more than a few seconds (else classify as nuke)

Through E: Yae, Albedo, Fischl, Raiden E, Morax E (lesser extent)
Through Q: Beidou, Xiangling, Xingqiu, Kazuha, Venti, Yoimiya, Ganyu, Ayato
Through ocean clam bubble pop: Qiqi

Term sub-dps sometimes refers to off field dps or burst dps.

On field dps

Through plunging: Xiao
Mainly CA: Itto, Ganyu, Hu Tao, Ayaka
CA/NA: Itto, Childe, Raiden
Mainly NA: Yoimiya, Eula, Diluc, Razor, Kokomi
Through E spam: Xiao C6

Through E: Childe, Hu Tao
Through Q: Xiao, Itto, Razor, Eula, Raiden, Kokomi

100% uptime (or near 100% uptime): Xiao, Diluc, Eula, Ganyu, Ayaka
Downtime: Raiden, Itto, Childe, Hu Tao, Yoimiya

Nukes

Single instance of damage with high scaling (e.g. around 700% scaling or more)

Eula, Raiden, Childe, Mona, Hu Tao, Jean, Morax, Aloy

Some bursts do DoT or are separated into multiple hits within a short time

Diluc, Qing, Ayaka, Chongyun

DMG Bonus self-buff

Xiao, Raiden, Mona, Kazuha C6, Yae (through EM passive)

Many of these characters get so much DMG Bonus that they benefit greatly from ATK buffs like Bennett.

ATK self-buff

Itto, Noelle, Hu Tao

They buff their own ATK by converting HP or DEF. They benefit more from DMG Bonus buffs like Mona or Kazuha.

HP scaling

Hu Tao, Morax, Kokomi

Tenacity set, HP weapons, HP sands

DEF scaling

Itto, Yun Jin, Gorou, Albedo

Husk set, DEF sands, DEF weapons

EM scaling

Kazuha, Yae Miko

Stance change

Childe, Raiden
