Chenyu vale

Chenyu vale will release in 4.4. It contains Qiaoying village (翘英庄). King George of the British empire is written 乔治 Qiaozhi in Chinese. And the 英 ying character can also symbolize Britain (England). Furina, Charlotte, George, Qiaoying.

Chenyu Adeptea is a product of the vale. Tea was introduced to Britain from China. Port situated close to Fontaine. Some of the names use Cantonese romanization, so perhaps the Chenyu Vale area is inspired by the southern coastal regions surrounding Hong Kong (and perhaps in general Southeast China).
