Silver Wolf (银狼) is a punklorde hacker, aether editor, user banner, bubblegum chewer, and stellaron hunter. What Silver Wolf does has no equivalent in Genshin: she can implant a weakness into the enemy. What this would mean in Genshin is adding an elemental gauge weakness to an enemy's shield or armor. So essentially a Fatui pyro shield could now take more gauge damage from say dendro attacks. But no character in Genshin can even do that.

Each of her attacks can debuff the enemy, reducing def, atk, or speed. Besides implanting a weakness, her skill also shreds res to the particular element by 20%. Moreover it will shred all res by an additional ~10%. So she takes the role of universal res shredder. Lastly, her ultimate will shred def by ~40% (can be leveled up).

Imagine this in Genshin: you have a character that shreds res with E (ok maybe Morax), shreds def, atk, speed with each attack (who does this?), and then shreds 40% def with Q all at C0.

By comparison, Lisa's Q shreds 15% def.

A 40% def shred is around a 1.25 times increase in damage in Genshin. This becomes magnified considering Silver Wolf's other attacks can further shred def, and her skill can shred res (up to ~30% for a single element and ~10% universal res shred). Now 30% res shred is also around a 1.22 times increase in damage. So multiply the def shred with res shred, and Silver Wolf can increase damage (for all allies) by more than 1.5 times.
