Fontaine Oceanid Theory

There is a fan theory circulating around that the people of Fontaine are actually similar to hydro eidolons or oceanids. They can combine form and split like other oceanids. Moreover, that is partly why they dissolve in primordial water whereas non-Fontaine people do not: because the bodies of native Fontaine people are different.
