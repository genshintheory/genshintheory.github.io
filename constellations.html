C6 ranking

This is focusing on the C6 itself, rather than assessing C0, C1, C2, ..., C6 altogether. For example, Raiden's C2/C3 is arguably more powerful than many C6 characters, but Raiden's C6 itself may not be as big an increase as her C3. But that does not change the fact that a C6 Raiden is just as powerful, if not more so than a C3 Raiden (although maybe not by much). Here we would be evaluating Raiden's C6 rather than a C6 Raiden.

Other constellations

Usually C6 stands out as the most powerful constellation in the kit. But sometimes earlier constellations can be very powerful as well. Some highlights include C2/C3 Raiden (ignore 60% def), C4 Jean (-40% anemo res), Chongyun's C2 (reducing cd by 15% within e), Xinyan's C2 (100% crit rate increase on q).

Note Aloy does not have constellations.
