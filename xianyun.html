Kit

Elemental Skill: Chasing Crane

Her E makes her jump into the air and enter the 'Cloud Transmogrification' state. It can be used 3 times and converts her plunging atk damage to driftcloud wave/chasing crane (this is leveled with her E). The more times the E is used the more her plunge does. Her E is also called skyladder (the part when she jumps in the air). The skyladder will deal anemo damage to enemies she hits while ascending (at most once per enemy). During her cloud state, she will not take fall damage.

E can be used midair. So this is an upgrade of kazuha.

The E generates 5 anemo particles. It has 12s cd. If she does not plunge during her cloud state, her the cd of her next E will be decreased by 3.
