Morax is also known as Vago Mundo (尘世闲游 chenshi xianyou). In this version, Morax ran with Eula (优菈 Youla). Moreover, the Serenitea Pot (尘歌壶) was released. Morax also provides a shield, similar to baizhu and Kirara (qiliangliang). This gives further evidence for a particular interpretation of Morax.

Morax's second story quest featuring Kun Jun, Jiu, and Azhdaha released.
