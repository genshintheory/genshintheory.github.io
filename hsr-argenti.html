Argenti (銀枝 pronounced Ginshi in Japanese) is a physical erudition unit.

Also, Argenti seems to make something more obvious, whereas Ayaka is a bit more subtle.

Kit

Argenti (銀枝 Ginshi) has two bursts, a 90 cost version and 180 cost version. Let's call them Q1, Q2. His E and Q are aoe. His Q2 has higher scaling than Q1. Moreover Q2 deals 6 extra instances of bounce damage to random enemies.

Also, for each enemy hit when he uses his basic atk, E, Q, he gains a stack of ascend and regenerates 3 energy. He can have up to 10 stacks, and each stack gives him more crit rate.

Ginshi in Genshin

There is currently no Genshin character with 2 different Q (that cost different energy). Childe's Q is 60 energy no matter which form he uses. Also there is no 'physical' vision per se in Genshin.

Ginshi E can do aoe damage. His Q1 can do an aoe nuke. His Q2 can do a bigger nuke.

For each enemy hit by AA/E/Q, he gains x energy and a stack of ascend, which increases his cr, up to 10 stacks. This effect can occur once per y seconds.
