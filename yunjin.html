Yun Jin (云堇) is a Geo polearm character more useful than Zhongli.

Talents

Her damage and buffs scale with her DEF. Her E is a counter like Beidou's. Talent priority is Q > E.

Constellations

All her constellations are quite good. Her C2, C3, C4, and C6 boost are the key ones that boost her support abilities.

Build

Husk (released in Version 2.3) 4 piece is her bis for boosting DEF and Geo DMG.

DEF/ER sands, DEF/Geo DMG goblet, DEF/CRIT circlet. Substats: DEF, CRIT > ER. Yun Jin ascends with ER.

5 stars: Homa, PJWS, Skyward Spine. 4 stars: Catch, Favonius Lance, Deathmatch.

Teams

She works well with Yoimiya, Eula, Itto. She can also support Diluc, Childe.
