Kit

Wrio is the first cryo catalyst introduced in the game.

Similar to Ayaka, Wrio applies cryo fast, so he is not usually the one melting. Supports: Shenhe, anemo vv, Bennett, hydro applicator for freeze (Yelan, XQ, etc.), Mona, Dehya, Yun Jin, Thoma, XL.

Kit seems similar to Scaramouche (E and Q). Q is a 5-hit nuke like Scaramouche. C2 gives dmg bonus to Q like Scaramouche.

His E (Icefang Rush) lasts 10s with a 16s cd (so 6s of downtime). Moreover his E gives a multiplicative buff to his NA (chilling penalty state). So far, similar to Scaramouche (and the multiplier buff is also similar to Yoimiya). But Wrio's E will also cause his NA to drain HP every .1s. This is not Scaramouche.

With his passive, when his hp dips below 60% during his E, his next CA (a sky uppercut) will deal 50% more dmg and heal 30% hp (gracious rebuke). This is actually quite unique among dps units. This effect occurs once per 5s, so this allows 2 buffed CA during his 10s E. He can use this buffed CA outside his E state too.

His E state (chilling penalty) is indicated by a cryo wolf hovering near him.

His other passive gives him 6% atk each time his hp changes during his E, up to 5 stacks for 30% atk buff.

Scaramouche and Noctis have range on their attacks, whereas Wrio seems to be more of a close-range melee fighter, suiting his boxing background.

Wrio is the British version of Scaramouche. (What's that supposed to mean?)
