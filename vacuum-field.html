Vacuum Field

Vacuum Fields (真空吸引场, Zhēnkōng Xīyǐn-chǎng, "Vacuum Suction Field"; 真空フィールド, Shinkuu Fiirudo)[1] are objects that can pull or suck in enemies and other objects within an AoE. This can be achieved via character talents like Venti's Wind's Grand Ode, The Viridescent Hunt weapon passive, or other special event effects. Vacuum Fields are a kind of Crowd Control that can group or clump mobs closer together, making it easier for the player to attack multiple enemies at the same time.

Pulling Strength

"Hunt a Cocogoat. Please and thank you."
Our community needs more information on this page! Can you help out? Click here to add more.

Some light enemies like slimes and hilichurls can be quickly pulled into vacuum fields and even lifted. Others will only slowly be dragged into the vortex.

From the source data, there appears to be two parameters governing the pulling strength of the vacuum field (also labeled "Wind Zones" in the source code):

  • Strength
  • Attenuation

It is unclear what exactly these refer to physically. It appears that the higher the Strength, the faster the pulling acceleration. Some of the Vacuum Field abilities will also target different entities (only enemies or things other than enemies). Some also depend on the weight of the enemy (see Interruption Resistance/Theoretical Data).

Affected Entities

The following entities can be pulled into (some or all) player-created vacuum fields:

  • Many enemies
  • Items (enemy drops)
  • Some wildlife like foxes and boars
  • Windblume Festival Commemorative Balloon
  • Some player-created entities:
    • Amber's Baron Bunny
    • Klee's Jumpy Dumpty (both the Bounce and the Mines)
    • Sayu's Muji-Muji Daruma
    • Xiangling's Guoba

The following entities cannot be pulled in by player-created vacuum fields:

  • NPCs
  • Bosses, including Weekly Bosses

Events

Some events have special effects or objects that can create vacuum fields that affect either the opponent or player.

  • Never-Ending Battle: Days 2-3 allow players to create vacuum fields under special conditions
  • Invitation of Windblume (Floral Freefall): the Vortex Balloon can create a small vacuum field in the air that can pull in the gliding player
  • Theater Mechanicus/2021-08-12: one of the upgrades for the Dust Devil will allow it to pull enemies within an AoE

Enemies

  • Anemo Specter: Upon defeat, if at max fury it will create a vacuum field that sucks in players and deals DoT.
  • Fatui Anemoboxer Vanguard: Upon infusing their shield with Pyro, Hydro, Cryo or Electro, they will start sucking the player in and punch them, reflecting the damage.
  • Eye of the Storm: Creates a vacuum field that sucks the player in and crashes into them from above.
  • Anemo Hypostasis: Creates multiple tornadoes and a vacuum area.
  • Maguu Kenki: Can create a vacuum field that drags the player towards it.
  • Ruin Destroyer: Can create a vacuum field that drags the player towards it.
