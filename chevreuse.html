Kit

She seems similar to Nilou. Similar to how Nilou needs 2-element dendro-hydro teams to buff bloom, Chevreuse needs 2-element pyro-electro teams (and overloaded) to buff the team. So in this sense, both Nilou and Chevreuse are harmony characters. However, Chevreuse's kit is not as restrictive as Nilou.

First, Chevreuse/Nilou's passive is not as restrictive as something like Lyney or Gorou/Itto, who want mono-element teams (3 of same element, where last slot is flex). But it is still more restrictive than say Noctis or Xiaogan's passives. So they are in a kind of middle ground in terms of team restriction (as opposed to characters with more or less 'complete freedom' in team-building such as Xiao, Scaramouche, Wrio, Furina, etc.).

Even then, Nilou is almost completely chained to needing only hydro and dendro for her golden chalice passive as that gives a big buff to bloom and enables her teams to do more damage. On the other hand, the only thing Chevreuse gets from pyro-electro only teams is her passive 40% res shred to pyro and electro. Everything else she does she can do outside of such teams. Even her C6 buffing dmg bonus does not require such a team restriction. As such, Chevreuse can be played in many more teams, outside pyro-electro only teams, compared to what Nilou's kit requires.

Teams

She can be played in: 1) Lyney teams with Kuki, Yae, 2) Raiden national teams with XL, Bennett. 3) Yoimiya-Raiden-Bennett teams.

Some theorycrafters say none of these teams would be optimal for dps as adding Yelan/XQ/Furina for overvape/vapeload and dendro would be a dps increase. There is perhaps one situation where the enemy has a constant hydro status, like the hydro tulpa/slime, where Chevreuse teams can be better (as hydro subdps will not deal damage to a hydro tulpa).
