DPS Calculations

We assume that with artifact stats and weapons, all characters have similar ATK, Crit Rate, Crit Damage, etc. Following calculations only focus on motion values / talent scalings. Below just gives some examples of dps calculations. See dps and team calcs for more details.

Xiao

Level 10 AA, Level 10 Q, High Plunge, no initial damage bonus

\( 404*1.952\approx 788.6 \)

Ganyu

Level 10 AA, no initial damage bonus

\( (230+392)/3=207.3 \)

This is lower than Xiao's high plunge scaling.

  • Xiao also attacks faster than Ganyu's charged shots.
  • Also Xiao high plunges have larger AoE (6m radius) than Ganyu's charged shot blooms (5m radius).
  • Ganyu's charged shots are actually split into two parts (the first part can miss entirely).

Conclusion:

  • Based on talent scaling, there is no way any Morgana team can outperform a simple Xiao-Bennett-Venti core team in DPS.
  • Even if you reduce Cryo resistance by 40% with Venti and Viridescent Venerer (from say 10% to -30% RES), that only gives a 1.15 RES multiplier meaning an MV of \(1.15*622\approx 715 \) which is still lower than Xiao. And even then the ATK buff from Bennett will widen the gap even further.

This is only at C0. At C6, Xiao widens the gap even further.

Notes:

  • Rosaria has higher charged attack scaling than Hu Tao.
  • Diluc E and Hu Tao CA have no ICD (or short enough ICD) so they can react on every strike.
  • Hu Tao is very single target. Even Diluc's claymore swings cover a wider AoE without costing stamina.
  • Xingqiu is single target, which makes him an inferior support compared to characters with better AoE.
  • Hu Tao has negative synergy with Bennett, unlike other Pyro characters, which ultimately makes Hu Tao a worse DPS. Moreover, until Thoma's release, it was very hard for Hu Tao to take advantage of an Anemo VV shred.
  • Hu Tao has negative synergy with C6 Zhongli (who heals) and would rather prefer Thoma as a shielder.
    • Zhongli is best suited as a support for Xiao who benefits from the Anemo RES debuff. Morgana does not need Zhongli. Childe-Xiangling does not need Zhongli. Hu Tao-Thoma does not need Zhongli. If you just use Zhongli for a shield, you can just run Diona or Thoma. If you want to shred RES with Zhongli, for most characters you can just use Anemo VV or Superconduct to reduce RES even more. Really, only the Anemo DPS Xiao benefits from the Anemo RES shred that Zhongli provides (in the game, it is very hard to reduce Anemo RES). Furthermore, C6 Zhongli is basically built for Xiao as it counters the health drain from Xiao's ultimate.

Other Pyro characters:

  • Yoimiya has the highest Normal Attack scaling among bow users. For Yoimiya, Yelan or XQ act as sub-dps rather than vape enablers. Vapeload (fireworks) Yoimiya with Yelan-Raiden/Yae is viable. The thing with overload is that it is a symmetric reaction (either pyro or electro can proc it), so one needs to be careful on whose EM is being factored into the calculation.
  • Childe is better for Xiangling than Xingqiu. Xiangling's Pyronado also has no ICD and has a scaling of 201 on each swing at Level 10 (becomes 238 at Level 13), which is lower than Hu Tao's charged attack. But Pyronado is AoE, can snapshot, does not cost stamina, and Childe will do more damage for Xiangling than Xingqiu for Hu Tao.
