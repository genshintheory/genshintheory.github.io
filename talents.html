Talent priority

Some characters need their whole kit to function optimally. Others only use parts of their kit, with some talents not even being used at all. And then there are some characters that care most about their passive talents (leveling their active talents not even necessary).

This can happen when the character provides a special ability like crowd control or buff via passive. The introduction of dendro and hyperbloom has made it so that many hyperbloom comps only need three elements to react to do damage (and could care less about talent level). This makes certain teams easy to build, if you do not have to care about talent level as much.
