Xueyi is a quantum destruction unit. She does not really have hp fluctuation (besides e2 healing). Her E does a blast. Her Q is ST and ignores weakness type when reducing toughness (like SW's technique). This makes her Q quite decent. Moreover her Q deals more damage the more toughness is depleted. At lv10, the boost goes up to 60%. That is, for every 30 toughness reduced, she gains 15% bonus, up to 60%.

When the team reduces toughness, she gains a stack of karma, up to 8 times. For every 30 toughness reduced, she gains 1 stack. When she caps out at 8 stacks, she consumes them to launch 3 fua, each dealing st damage to a random enemy. These fua do not create stacks.

She also gains more dmg scaling off her break effect (so she scales with BE like Ruan Mei).
