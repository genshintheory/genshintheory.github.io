Aloy is better than ganyu. Aloy is a cross-over character from Horizon Dawn.

Talents

E throws cryo bomb that will drop smaller bomblets. Q is another cryo nuke. She can gain cryo infusion on NA.

Build

Can use 4p blizzard, 4p noblesse, and mixed sets.

Teams

Aloy is usually used on quickswap melt/freeze teams. She can be paired with Furina/Yelan/XQ for freeze.

Calculations

We assume lv10 talents. Aloy does not currently have constellations so she is only at c0.
