Genshin equivalent

If sp is the resource of HSR, then time is a resource in Genshin. In HSR, skills cost sp to use. In Genshin, all E and Q have cd, taking time before they can be used again.

Genshin does not have an sp system, so to design an equivalent of Sparkle in Genshin, such a character may have to do something equivalent to what sp economy can do for HSR characters. In HSR, more sp allows characters to use their skills more (and some characters to use enhanced abilities that cost multiple sp). Things that cost more sp (to deal more dmg for example) take time to regenerate before using again. This corresponds to longer cd for abilities that deal damage.

For example, Alhaitham can hold E and plunge to get 3 mirrors instantly and frontload his damage. His E has 18s cd, and his CA/PA mirror has 12s cd, so it would take 18s before he can get 3 mirrors again (unless using Q). However, if he does not frontload his damage and say CA/PA for 1 mirror, then the cd is shorter at 12s. Moreover, by using E for 2 mirrors and doing dps for 8s, and then CA/PA for 1 mirror to dps for another 4s, he is on field for 12s, and he can use his E again after 6s.

So it seems that one way sp can be translated into Genshin is via cooldowns.

When Sparkle gives 4 sp to the team via her Q, it is almost like resetting the cd of all teammates' abilities in Genshin. So each teammate can use their E, Q one more time.

Sparkle can also increase the sp cap from 5 to 7. It is not currently known how this can be translated into Genshin mechanics.

Her E also buffs an ally's crit dmg based on her own. She can also buff dmg bonus.
