Wanderer (流浪者 Liulangzhe), also known as Scaramouche (散兵 Sanbing), Balladeer, Kunikuzushi (国崩 Guobeng), Kabukimono (倾奇者 Qingqizhe), Shouki no Kami (正机之神 Zhengji zhi shen), Everlasting Lord of Arcane Wisdom, Mr. Bohemian Rhapsody, Mr. Fandango, is the 6th of the harbingers.

Talents

Wanderer's NA and CA have good AoE. His E allows him to levitate and gives him a blue stamina bar. At the end of his E (when he runs out of blue stamina), he can do a plunge attack for an extra instance of damage.

His E is one of the most unique abilities in the game and supports the hypothesis that the fatui harbingers have unique tricks up their sleeve (Childe had riptide or 断流 duanliu, which is an interesting name in its own right).

One of Scara's passives buffs him in E mode when he swirls with different elements: buffing his atk, crit rate, energy, or stamina duration.

Another of his passives gives him a certain probability to not consume air stamina when sprinting midair while also firing additional wind arrows.
