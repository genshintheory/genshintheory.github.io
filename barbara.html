Barbara is a better hydro healer than kokomi. Her E healing has longer duration at 15s. With her passive this can increase to 20s (if the party obtains energy particles). With her C2 her cd is reduced by 15% from 32s to 27.2s, giving her a 7.2s downtime (compared to kokomi's 8s downtime).

Her Q heals the whole party, without having to stay on-field and NA as with kokomi. And her C6 can revive fallen party members, something kokomi can never do.

Her C2 also gives 15% hydro dmg so she can be paired with hydro dps like Childe, Yelan, etc.

Weapons that can be used are the TTDS, prototype amber, ER catalysts, hp catalysts. Apparently dps Barbara is a thing, so perhaps dps catalysts like widsith, kagura can work.
