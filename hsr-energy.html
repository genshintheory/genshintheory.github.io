The min energy is 90 (Natasha), and the max energy for most characters is 140 (Jingliu, Yanqing). And then Argenti has a unique energy mechanic and can store up to 180 (double burst).

Using basic attacks, E, Q can generate energy. It seems that unlike Genshin, in HSR characters can gain energy from getting hit by enemies. This introduces the following idea for Genshin: have a character generate energy from getting hit, taking damage, or draining hp. Similar to how Marechaussee gives crit from hp fluctuation, it should not be too hard to design a passive, kit, artifact that gives more energy from hp fluctuation.
