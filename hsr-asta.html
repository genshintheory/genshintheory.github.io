Asta in Genshin

Asta E does bounce damage that hits up to 5 enemies. For each enemy hit, Asta increases all party members' atk by 10%. Effect lasts 15s. E cd is 10s. This alone can make Asta broken, a kind of Bennett but with E.

Now Genshin speed is different from the speed in the turn-based HSR. Instead of buffing speed/advancing action, in Genshin, Asta Q can reduce all party members' E and Q cd by 5s.
