Simulated Universe (SU) is a form of endgame in HSR. It appears eerily similar to the dungeon event introduced in version 2.2 of Genshin.

General remarks

In world 3 with the fight against Gepard, selecting the path of preservation helps. In world 4 with the Svarog fight, destruction can help (a powerful shield/healing will not save you from being trapped by one of Svarog's hands; survivability is important here). In the world with the Kafka fight, abundance can help.

In one of the rooms, there is a chance to get all the blessings of one path (such as elation). Such a buff can be quite broken.

Progress to the point where you can start selecting path resonances (1-2). This will help a lot with boss fights.

Two nonplayable paths are present: remembrance (buff freeze) and elation (buff follow-up attacks). Erudition and harmony are not present.

Nice buffs: recover skill point when using follow up attack (elation), increase eidolon level (curio), flowing mist (upgrading gives chance to recover sp)
