The Daoguang emperor (道光帝) of the Qing dynasty was born Aisin Gioro Mianning (爱新觉罗绵宁). Put Ning and Guang together and you get Ningguang. Daoguang was also the emperor that lost to Alexandrina Victoria's British Empire in the Opium Wars.

Does Ningguang look like a character that would smoke opium? Maybe.

Besides that, Ningguang may be a reference to a certain novel.

In the game, Ningguang is known for the Jade Chamber, the floating palace in the sky.
