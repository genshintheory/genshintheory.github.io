Heralded as Geo Bennett, Miss Hina is a geo bow user whose niche is buffing def and geo damage.

Talents

Talent priority: E. Leveling Q is not as necessary unless you care about damage. The E gives a DEF buff and has 100% uptime (10s cd, 10s duration). His Q costs 80 energy, so running an ER sands or weapon can be common.

One passive buffs the team def by 25% for 12s after he uses his Q. Another passive buffs his own E and Q damage based on his DEF. His Q damage scales with his DEF like Albedo's E.

His C4 allows his Q to heal based on his DEF. His C6 allows his E and Q to buff geo crit dmg by 40%.

Fun fact: after using his NA Ripping Fang Fletching, he will growl.

Build

For artifacts, one can use 4p husk (for Q damage and C4 healing), 2p emblem for ER, and hybrid builds. Desired substats are ER, DEF, crit.

Best weapons include Elegy (for ER) and Favonius.
