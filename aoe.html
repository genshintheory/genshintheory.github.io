Elemental Reactions

Some Elemental Reactions have AoE effects:[1][2]

  • Superconduct does AoE Cryo DMG within a 5 meter radius
  • Swirl spreads elemental auras within a 6 meter radius
  • Overloaded creates an AoE explosion within a 5 meter radius

Other Notes:

  • The Plunging Attack AoE is usually less than 5 meters. During Xiao's ultimate, his Plunging AoE is increased up to ~6 meters for high plunges.[13]
  • Zhongli's stele has a height of ~4m and a resonance radius of ~8m, slightly less than the radius of Albedo's Solar Isotoma (~10m). The vertical resonance range is also around 8m, measured from the base of the stele.[14]
  • The AoE for many other abilities such as claymore/polearm swings, catalyst charged attacks, etc. is usually within a 3m radius.
  • The auto-targetting range for some bow and catalyst users (such as Diona, Yanfei or Kokomi) is 15 meters.
