Overview

Burgeon teams do not seem to be as profuse as hyperbloom teams due to the absence of a pyro Raiden/Kuki (with the exception of Dehya). Many pyro units can cause burning when paired with dendro. And sometimes, the pyro-dendro combo is just used for burning for melt teams, rather than burgeon.

But some burgeon teams include:

  • Nahida-double hydro-Thoma/XL/Xinyan: Thoma and XL can run into ER issues, and Thoma is usually better as XL can apply too much pyro with E and Q. The key is that the pyro app is on the Q, costs energy and faces downtime, which can make these kind of burgeon teams less convenient than a hyperbloom team that just uses Raiden or Kuki E. Xinyan E would need to hit multiple enemies.
  • Nahida / Xingqiu / Beidou / Thoma: a 'rainbow' team
  • Nahida / Xingqiu / Kuki / Thoma
  • Childe / Nahida / Kuki / Thoma, Xiangling, Xinyan.
  • Childe / Nahida / Bennet / Kazuha
