Best DPS characters

Here we say a hypercarry is a character that does the vast majority of the team's damage (like 2/3 or more). A carry is a character that does the majority of the team's damage (1/2 or more). There can be on field and off field carries. Supports are those that buff other people's damage (not necessarily doing much damage themselves). Sub-DPS is an off field character that provides a significant portion of the team dps. Off field carries are sub-dps by definition. DPS is usually used to refer to on field carries, though Sub-DPS can also be on field if they choose to stay on field. See DPS ranking for a more thorough list.
